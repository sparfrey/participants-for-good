Our commitments

People and communities are not a resource to be mined. These are non-negotiable.

🧾

Open books

We publish how much money went to participants and nonprofits, every year, publicly.

💵

Fair pay, always

Participants are paid for every session. Contributions to causes come from the fee — never out of anyone's pocket.

✋

Consent first

Plain-language consent for every study. Participants own their data and can withdraw anytime.

🌱

No research fatigue

We cap how often anyone is asked to participate, and nonprofits control how their name is used.
