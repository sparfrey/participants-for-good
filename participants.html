How it works

A few minutes to join, and every study after that does double duty.

1

Sign up

Tell us a little about yourself so we can match you with studies that fit. Takes a few minutes.

2

Pick your cause

Choose the charity or nonprofit your participation supports: a partner organization or one you bring. Change it anytime.

3

Join studies that fit

Interviews, usability tests, and surveys. Most take 30–60 minutes, online or near you.

4

Everyone gets paid

You're paid for every completed session, and your cause receives a contribution on top.

What studies are like

No trick questions, no tests you can fail. Companies just want your honest experience.

What you might do

  • Try a new app and say what's confusing
  • A 30-minute video call about how you shop, bank, or get around
  • Short surveys you can do from your phone
  • Occasional in-person sessions, always paid

What you can count on

  • Plain-language consent before every study
  • Skip any question, leave any time, still no cost to you
  • Your data belongs to you, never sold
  • We limit how often you're asked, so it never becomes a chore
💚

Your pay is yours. The giving is extra.

The contribution to your cause comes from the company's recruiting fee, never out of your pay. You always receive the full incentive for every session you complete.
